Our Story

Creating Unforgettable
Celebrations

For over a decade, Ifico Heritage Party Palace & Banquet has been the crown jewel of Kathmandu Valley's event scene. Nestled in the heart of Balkumari, Lalitpur, we have crafted thousands of moments that live forever in the hearts of our guests.

Our philosophy is simple — every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. From the grandest weddings to intimate anniversaries, our team of seasoned event professionals orchestrates every detail with passion, precision, and uncompromising elegance.
