About Me

Where Technology Meets Creativity

I'm Sobisha Agrawal, a BScIT student at Presidential Graduate School in Kathmandu, Nepal — actively building expertise in cybersecurity, UI/UX design, and front-end development.

Technology fascinates me because it's the infrastructure of everything we do online. My primary interest lies in information security and cybersecurity — understanding how systems are protected, where vulnerabilities exist, and how to design safer digital experiences.

Alongside my technical education, I create authentic content that connects brands with real audiences — a skill that has sharpened my communication, visual thinking, and understanding of digital behavior. These two worlds complement each other more than people expect.

My goal is to graduate as a capable IT professional with a specialization in cybersecurity, while carrying forward the creativity and digital fluency I've built through content creation.
