FAQ

Helpful details before your visit.

Can we customize decoration and stage styling?

Yes. The team can adapt floral styling, stage layout, lighting, entry decor, and table settings to the event type and package.

Do you support both vegetarian and non-vegetarian menus?

Yes. Menus can be built with vegetarian, non-vegetarian, traditional, and mixed buffet selections.

Is parking available for large events?

Yes. Parking support is available for guests, with arrival coordination for larger wedding and reception bookings.

How early should we book?

For weddings and peak dates, booking three to six months in advance gives the best hall and package availability.
