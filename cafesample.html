Our Philosophy

More Than
a Café

Founded in 2016 by Avi Shrestha and Sobi Agrawal, Avi's began as a quiet conviction: that Nepal grows some of the world's most extraordinary coffee — and that it deserves to be celebrated, not overlooked.

Our beans come from the misty highlands of Gulmi, Lamjung, Arghakhanchi, and Kaski — sourced directly from 14 smallholder farming families we've known for years. Every harvest, our team travels to cup and hand-select each lot.

At our Patan roastery, head roaster Dipesh Maharjan develops individual roast profiles for each origin — honouring the terroir that makes Nepali coffee so unique.

From Farm to Cup

The Art of Nepali Coffee

Every cup is the result of hundreds of intentional decisions — made with science, love, and deep respect for Nepal's land.

01
🌱

Origin & Sourcing

We work directly with 14 smallholder farms across Gulmi, Lamjung, Kaski, Arghakhanchi, Palpa, and Rolpa. Our team visits each harvest to cup and select lots by hand.

02
🔥

Artisan Roasting

At our Patan roastery, head roaster Dipesh Maharjan crafts individual roast profiles per lot — balancing sweetness, body, and origin character with obsessive precision.

03
⚗️

Precision Brewing

Every brew method is calibrated daily. Himalayan spring water at 93°C. Grind size dialled per gram. Extraction windows measured in seconds. Never guessed.

04
🌿

Sustainability

Zero-waste packaging, solar-powered roasting, and a closed-loop compost programme that returns spent grounds to our partner farms as fertiliser.
