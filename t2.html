FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for Nepal?+

Yes, entry visas are required. Tourist visas can be processed efficiently on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport (TIA) in Kathmandu via automatic processing kiosks.

What is the best season to visit?+

The primary high tracking windows occur between September to November (Autumn) and March to May (Spring) for crystal-clear sky environments and stable pass temperatures.

Are your tours customizable?+

Yes, all routes can be tailored. You can tweak destination sequences, upgrade mountain tea houses to premium mountain eco-resorts, or insert helicopter legs.

Do you provide airport pickup?+

Yes, every itinerary booked includes private traditional greetings and vehicle transport options from the airport directly to your hotel base.

Are trekking permits included?+

Yes, all TIMS logistics, conservation area entrance documents, and municipal local taxes are systematically pre-arranged within our standard quote prices.
