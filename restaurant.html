Our Story

Crafting Memorable
Dining Experiences

✦

Nestled in the vibrant lanes of Thamel, Himalayan Durbar was born from a passion for authentic Nepali flavors elevated to fine dining. Since 2009, we have been the culinary heart of Kathmandu, welcoming guests from across Nepal and the world.

Our kitchen blends centuries-old recipes from the Himalayas with contemporary culinary techniques, creating a dining journey that honours tradition while embracing innovation.

Chef's Philosophy

Every dish tells the story of Nepal's diverse regions — from the spice trails of the Terai to the pristine flavors of the high mountains.

Farm-to-Table Commitment

We source seasonal produce directly from local Nepali farmers, ensuring freshness in every bite and supporting our communities.

Meet Our
Master Chef

✦

Chef Bikram Shrestha trained at the Culinary Institute of Asia in Bacolod before honing his skills across restaurants in London and Tokyo. Returning to Nepal with a renewed passion for his roots, he transformed Himalayan Durbar into an internationally acclaimed destination.

His philosophy is simple: the mountains give us everything we need. Every dish is a meditation on the Himalayan landscape — clean, bold, and deeply nourishing.
