Our Story

The Art of Specialty Coffee

Founded in 2009, Veloura Café began as a quiet obsession with a single perfect cup. Today, it's a destination for those who believe the details matter.

We source our beans from single-origin farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — relationships we've cultivated over more than a decade. Every batch is roasted in-house within 72 hours of serving, ensuring flavour profiles that most cafés simply cannot offer.

Our baristas are trained artisans. Each espresso is dialled in fresh each morning. Each latte is poured with intention. From the grind to the cup, every element is considered — because we believe that exceptional coffee is not an accident, but a discipline.

Our Process

From Seed to
Your Cup

Every great cup begins long before it reaches your hands. We've built relationships with farmers across three continents, and we believe in full transparency across our entire supply chain.

01
Origin & Sourcing

We work directly with smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Twice-yearly origin trips ensure quality and fair compensation at every level.

02
In-House Roasting

Our 15kg Loring Kestrel roaster is dialled in weekly. We roast to accentuate each origin's natural sweetness, never masking character with dark roast.

03
Rest & Degassing

All espresso rests for 7–10 days post-roast. Filter roasts for 2–4 days. Rushing this step is the industry's most common mistake. We don't.

04
Precision Extraction

Daily dialling in. Slayer espresso machines calibrated to ±0.1g dose. Water at 93°C. EK43 grinders. Everything matters, and we act accordingly.
