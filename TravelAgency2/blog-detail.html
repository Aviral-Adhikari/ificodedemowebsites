When to go

March-May and September-November offer the clearest balance of weather, visibility and route reliability. Winter is quieter and excellent for cultural travel.

Route planning

Combine Kathmandu heritage, Pokhara relaxation and a short Himalayan view route if you have one week. Add Chitwan, Mustang or Everest region when your schedule allows.

Comfort tips

Private transfers, carefully timed flights and pre-booked quality stays change the rhythm of a Nepal journey dramatically. Pack layers and keep key days flexible.

Booking advice

Confirm inclusions, permits, guide credentials and cancellation rules before paying. Localifys team can review your route and turn it into a polished itinerary.
