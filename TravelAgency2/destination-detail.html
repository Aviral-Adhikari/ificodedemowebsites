Destination guide

Kathmandu Valley

UNESCO squares, quiet courtyards, mountain views and a living culture layered through every lane.

Overview

Kathmandu Valley is the ideal starting point for Nepal, combining temples, museums, food walks, artisan workshops and short hillside escapes.

Attractions

SwayambhunathBoudhanathPatan Durbar SquareBhaktapurNagarkot sunriseAson market

Activities

Heritage walks, cooking classes, monastery visits, photography tours, wellness treatments and day hikes.

Weather

Best months are March-May and September-November. Winter is crisp and clear; monsoon brings lush landscapes and occasional delays.

Map

Travel tips

  • Carry small cash for heritage entries and markets.
  • Dress respectfully at temples and monasteries.
  • Plan old-city visits early to avoid traffic.
